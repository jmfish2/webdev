Biography of Jason M Fisher

Early Life

As the son of a father who was in the United States Airforce, Jason grew up all over the United States. His early life was constantly meeting new and interesting people. He learned early that the greatest gift in life is a conversation with a complete stranger, and that with just a little listening, everyone on this planet is connected.

Miltary Service

Jason wished to serve his country, so he joined the United States Navy. He trained and completed Naval Nuclear Power Training as a Reactor Operator. School was not easy, but he learned that persistence is a key factor to success as much as and more than raw talent.

Now

He is the father to his three children and loving husband to his wife. He is an avid runner, crossfit fanatic, and yet loves to play a good board game with friends. He finds life amazing and feels blessed every day he is amongst the living.
